Our Story

Welcome to our pottery shop! We're a family-owned business located in the heart of Morocco, and we're passionate about preserving the country's rich history of pottery making. Our shop is dedicated to bringing you the best quality pottery, handmade by our skilled artisans who have honed their craft for generations.

Our pottery is made using the finest materials available, including local clay and natural dyes. Each piece is carefully crafted by hand, ensuring that no two are exactly alike. From our colorful tagines to our intricately designed bowls, our pottery is a celebration of Moroccan culture and heritage.

Handmade with love, crafted to perfection.

Inspired by Morocco, made for you.

Nestled in the winding streets of Sale in Morocco, there is a shop that holds within its walls a world of wonder. As you approach the shop, the warmth of the sun-kissed clay that makes up its walls emanates through the air, and you feel as though you are being drawn in by an invisible force. This is a place where tradition meets innovation, and the past blends effortlessly with the present.

Authentic Moroccan pottery

Inside our shop, you are immediately greeted by an array of colors, patterns, and textures that seem to dance together in a beautiful symphony. The pottery on display is a feast for the eyes, with each piece telling its own unique story. You can feel the passion and dedication that has gone into each and every creation, and it is as though you have stepped into a world of magic.

100% Handmade

When it comes to creating something truly special, there's no substitute for the human touch. That's why we're proud to say that all of our pottery is 100% handmade by skilled artisans who have dedicated their lives to the craft. From shaping the clay to adding the finishing touches, each piece is created with a level of care and attention that simply can't be replicated by machines.

Versatility

Whether you're looking for something to add a touch of elegance to your dining room or a playful piece to brighten up your kitchen, we have something for every taste and style. Our artisans are constantly experimenting with new shapes, sizes, and designs, pushing the boundaries of what is possible with clay. From intricate patterns to bold colors, each piece is a unique expression of creativity and imagination. And because our pottery is designed to be both functional and beautiful, you can use it every day without ever sacrificing style for practicality.
